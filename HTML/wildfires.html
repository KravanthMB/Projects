Wildfires

A wildfire, also known as a wildland fire, forest fire, grass fire, or bushfire, is an uncontrolled fire in an area of combustible vegetation that occurs in rural areas. Wildfires can cause extensive damage to property and human life, and also affect the natural environment and wildlife.
